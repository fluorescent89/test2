Research

My research is divided in two main sections. In the first part, I use observational data to analyze and understand the vulnerability of global supply networks against disruptions. For this purpose, I collect data from structured data sources such as Bloomberg, WRDS, FactSets and EIKON as well as unstructured data sources from the web and social media with predictive analytics, text mining, and network science. In the second part, I use optimization techniques to design supply networks which are efficient during normal time yet resilient during crisis.
